I wrote a post every day for the month of January, and I think it was so demanding that it wiped me out for six months. But I don't regret doing it; it was a nice little experiment with zero loss.

My plan for the future is to get back into the habit of writing something and putting it up on the internet. I'd say there's about a 55% chance of this happening for real. There's a lot of things I want to talk about, but crystallizing all those jumbled thoughts feel like pure labor.

On the other hand, I've been reading tons and tons. I've read 13 books this year (this is big for me!), not counting all the papers and articles and Substack posts I devour every morning. Sometimes I wonder if I could use my time more wisely. Like, vacuum the stairs or whatever.
