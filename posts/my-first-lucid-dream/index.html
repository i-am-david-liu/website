My first lucid dream

Had my first lucid dream on Friday. I woke up earlier than usual, so I went back to sleep.

I found myself suspended over a city. It was a lot darker than the linked picture, and almost none of the windows were lit. The ones that were shone brightly, like flat golden rectangles. Not a single thing moved.

I thought to myself, "hey, this feels pretty weird." Then I realized I was aware of my thoughts.

At this point I got super excited because I've never had a lucid dream before. And since I had zero prior experience, I jumped straight in without maintaining control over my dream state. The first (and only) action I took was fly headfirst into a skyscraper. I didn't care what would happen next; I just wanted to exert my newly appointed agency.

As I approached the building, I heard a deep rumbling noise, and then everything faded to black. I awoke shortly after.

After a bit of research, I conclude that I probably carried out a Wake-Initiated Lucid Dream (WILD). This is when you sleep for about 4-6 hours, wake up briefly, and go back to bed while cultivating a lucid dream state. Needless to say, I wasn't aware of this "cultivating" process, so I must have done it by accident.
